## Quick Reference: R2 Bucket URLs

Replace all `lh3.googleusercontent.com/aida-public/` URLs in App.tsx
with URLs from the marcjoy-web R2 bucket once images are uploaded.

Pattern: `https://YOUR_R2_PUBLIC_URL/[section]/[filename].webp`
